Medicio Bootstrap Theme For XOOPS CMS 2.5.8.x
=====

![Theme Preview](/theme_preview.png)

Medicio is a landing page template designed for medical & health industry such as doctors, clinics, dentists and another medical & health service company. This template has 4 variations on above the fold area slider, opt in form, video and call to action, also coming with 10 predefined template colors/skins

Ported to XOOPS [CMS 2.5.8.x](http://xoops.org/modules/news/article.php?storyid=6762)
